About
Me

  • Born in December, 1993.

The best way to describe myself would be to call me a nerd/geek: I love computers, technology and sciences; I don't socialize well, suck at sports, and in general being "cool."

I got into the computer scene at the age of 10 and soon caught interested in programming and web-development. I also got a glimpse of linux and got hooked.

I have been experimenting with and learning a lot of things since then: Photoshop, Flash, Visual Basic, hacking Windows XP, and Linux. I have been developing for the Web eversince I found it, slowly learning HTML, CSS, Javascript and PHP, through practice and trial and error.

Most of my projects from back then were for learning purposes and didn't see the light of day. This is something that happens to me still. Every project I start is mostly fired by curiosity to learn something specific and after I've mastered that the project is usually abandoned (note that this is only for my personal projects ;)).

Although most of my project background consists of web-development, I do not want to become a professional web-developer. I have, for some years now, fallen absolutely and hopelessly in love with Linux, and that is where I want my career to be. I dream of becomming a kernel developer. I am an avid FOSS enthusiast.

In my free time I usually watch movies and series or read novels. I like science fiction, fantacy, mistery, and everything nerdy. Some of my favorite series are Game of Thrones, Doctor Who, Sherlock and Cosmos. I also like sitcoms like Friends and The Big Bang Theory.
Some of the authors I love are H. G. Wells, Jules Verne, Gabriel Gracias Marquez , J. K. Rowling, G. R. R. Martin, John Green.
I don't think it's possible to list all my favorite movies here :).
My musical taste is difficult to explain: if I like it, I like it.
I also spend an aweful lot of time on YouTube. I watch Vsauce, MinutePhysics, MinuterEarth, CGP Grey, Smarter Every Day, Numberphile, Computerphile, Sixty Symbols, SciShow, Crash Course, vlogbrothers, et al.

I do not play any sports, the only sport I understand is Football (the gameplay, not the whole happenings in the European football clubs). I never really got into Computer Games, so the only games I play now are the one in my phone.
