Hello. I am a regular geek from Nepal. I love to work on the computer and everything about it. I have been into web-development since I was 10. One good/bad thing about me is that I usually do not publish anything untill it is perfect (it's a good excuse for so many of my unpublished projects ;)).
I am more interested in web-programming, or any kind of programming than designing.

My primary source of knowledge is practice, so when I am working on my personal projects my primary focus is on learning than actually completing the project. You always hear the advice to never re-invent the wheel, but I believe we can not actually master anything if we don't do everything from scratch (as far as practicable). So when I'm working on any personal project I usually do not use any of other people's code. Oviously, I learned this strategy fails for any real project, but I still hang on to this when I'm learning something new.
